Ed Gaskins has a diverse civil practice involving business problem solving, commercial litigation, administrative law, and valorem tax appeals, appellate advocacy as well as personal injury and wrongful death litigation. Ed is a native of North Carolina and has practiced his entire career in Wake County except for two years' service in the U.S. Army, including a year in Vietnam. Ed serves as the Firm's managing partner as well as its senior trial lawyer.
